DLF Independent Floors At DLF City Phase II, Gurgaon exudes opulence in terms of contemporary architecture and world-class design. The project is designed by international architects that make sure you get a world-class experience by living here. These magnificent abodes offer ample natural light in each corner of the flat. You will find spacious balconies and unobstructed natural views in each flat.


With DLF Independent Floors At DLF City Phase II Gurgaon, you get:


Top-quality fixtures and fittings

ISI marked electrical wire fittings. 

Wooden floorings in bedroom and living room.

Anti-Skid tiles in washrooms

Large glass windows

Olympic size swimming pool


DLF Independent Floors At DLF City Phase II MG Road Gurgaon is strategically located in one of the posh locations of Gurgaon. The location enjoys splendid public transportation facilities and is well connected with landmark projects. DLF Independent Floors At DLF City Phase II MG Road Gurgaon is located in close proximity with MG Road and NH-8. Indira Gandhi International Airport is hardly 10 km (15 min drive) away from the location.
